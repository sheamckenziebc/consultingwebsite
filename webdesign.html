Common Questions

Answers to frequently asked questions about my web design services

Why is there no monthly fee?

By using GitHub Pages for hosting and building efficient static websites, there's no need for expensive servers or complicated content management systems. This allows me to offer websites with no recurring costs, saving you money in the long run.

How do I update my website?

You have several options. I can teach you how to make simple updates yourself, you can contact me for updates as needed (additional cost), or we can set up a simple content management system for more frequent updates (additional cost).

Do I really own everything?

Absolutely. Your domain is registered in your name, and you have full ownership of all website files and code. I can provide the complete GitHub repository so you have everything, giving you true ownership and portability.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most simple websites can be completed within 1-2 weeks from our initial consultation. More complex projects may take longer depending on content preparation and revision cycles.
