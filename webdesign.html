Common Questions

Answers to frequently asked questions about my web design services

Why use GitHub Pages instead of WordPress?

GitHub Pages offers free, reliable hosting for static websites. Unlike WordPress, there are no monthly hosting fees, no security vulnerabilities from plugins, and pages load significantly faster. For most small business websites, a static site provides everything you need without the overhead.

Do I own my website and domain?

Absolutely. Your domain is registered in your name, and you have full ownership of all website files and code. I can provide the complete GitHub repository so you have everything, giving you true ownership and portability.

What happens after my included updates are used?

After your included updates are used, additional updates are available at $50/hour. Many clients find their included updates sufficient for the first year. I can also teach you how to make basic content updates yourself if you prefer.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks from our initial consultation. Basic sites can be completed in as little as one week, while more complex projects may take longer depending on content preparation and revision cycles.
