About Me

Strategic Development Professional focused on delivering results

My Background

I am a Strategic Development Professional excited by the power of emergent technologies, particularly the transformative potential of generative and agentic AI, to deliver results quickly and reliably. As a well-rounded generalist, I'm comfortable upskilling into any domain that moves a project forward. My work blends rigorous research with adaptable execution, a skill set I am constantly working to improve upon.

I believe the future hinges on agility: smaller, nimble teams that stay ahead of rapid technological shifts. My background in Political Science from the University of Victoria provides a strong foundation in analytical thinking, while my experience in hospitality management instills a practical, results-driven approach to problem-solving. Grounded in first-principles thinking, I cut through the noise to identify the next best step.
