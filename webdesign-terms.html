Web Design Terms of Service

Legal terms governing website design services

1. PARTIES AND SERVICES

This agreement ("Agreement") is made between Shea McKenzie ("Provider"), a sole proprietor operating in British Columbia, Canada, and the client ("Client") who engages Provider's web design services.

Provider offers website design, development, and maintenance services as outlined in the service packages on the Provider's website at sheamckenzie.com/webdesign.html.

2. TERM AND RENEWAL

2.1 The initial term of this Agreement is one (1) year from the date of website launch ("Initial Term").

2.2 This Agreement will automatically renew for successive one-year periods (each a "Renewal Term") unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

2.3 Renewal fees will be invoiced thirty (30) days prior to the renewal date at the then-current rates for the selected service package.

3. WEBSITE OWNERSHIP AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

3.1 Upon full payment of all fees due, Client shall own all rights, title, and interest in the website design, content, and code created specifically for Client.

3.2 Provider retains ownership of any pre-existing templates, code, or other materials used in the development of Client's website that were not created specifically for Client.

3.3 Provider may include a small credit link in the website footer stating "Website by Shea McKenzie" with a link to Provider's website. Client may request removal of this credit.

3.4 Provider may use Client's website in Provider's portfolio unless Client specifically requests otherwise in writing.

4. DOMAIN REGISTRATION AND OWNERSHIP

4.1 Domain names registered by Provider on behalf of Client shall be registered in Client's name, making Client the legal owner of the domain.

4.2 Provider will manage domain renewals during the term of this Agreement, with renewal costs passed through to Client without markup.

4.3 Provider will provide Client with access to the domain registrar account upon request.

4.4 Client is responsible for maintaining accurate contact information for domain registration purposes.

5. HOSTING AND GITHUB PAGES

5.1 Provider will host Client's website on GitHub Pages at no additional cost during the term of this Agreement.

5.2 Provider makes no warranties regarding GitHub Pages' uptime, availability, or continued free service, as these are controlled by GitHub, Inc.

5.3 If GitHub Pages changes its terms or ceases to be a viable hosting option, Provider will work with Client to find an alternative hosting solution, which may incur additional costs.

6. CONTENT UPDATES

6.1 Each service package includes a specified number of content updates per year as detailed on Provider's website.

6.2 Content updates are defined as modifications to text, images, or other content elements that do not require structural changes to the website.

6.3 Updates beyond the included allocation will be billed at $50 CAD per hour.

6.4 Unused updates do not roll over to subsequent years.

7. DOMAIN TRANSFER PROCESS

7.1 Client may request transfer of domain management away from Provider at any time by providing written notice.

7.2 Upon receiving a transfer request, Provider will:

  1. Unlock the domain for transfer within five (5) business days;
  2. Provide the authorization/EPP code required for transfer;
  3. Assist Client in updating the domain's administrative contact information;
  4. Provide reasonable assistance to facilitate a smooth transfer.

7.3 Client is responsible for initiating the transfer process with the receiving registrar.

7.4 Provider will not charge any fees for domain transfers.

8. WEBSITE FILES AND CODE

8.1 Client has the right to request a complete copy of all website files and code at any time.

8.2 Upon request, Provider will provide Client with:

  1. Access to the GitHub repository containing all website files;
  2. A downloadable ZIP archive of all website files;
  3. Documentation regarding the website structure, as available.

8.3 Provider will maintain a backup of Client's website files for the duration of this Agreement.

9. TERMINATION

9.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice.

9.2 Upon termination:

  1. Provider will provide Client with all website files and code;
  2. Provider will assist with domain transfer if requested;
  3. Client will be responsible for finding alternative hosting;
  4. Provider will remove the website from GitHub Pages within thirty (30) days of termination.

9.3 No refunds will be provided for prepaid services unless termination is due to Provider's material breach of this Agreement.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

10.1 Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the amount paid by Client for services in the twelve (12) months preceding any claim.

10.2 Provider shall not be liable for any indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages.

10.3 This limitation of liability reflects the allocation of risk between the parties and is an essential element of the basis of the bargain between them.

11.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Province of British Columbia and the federal laws of Canada applicable therein.

11.2 Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively in the courts of British Columbia.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

12.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, or negotiations.

12.2 This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
