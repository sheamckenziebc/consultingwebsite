Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information

1. Introduction

Welcome to Shea McKenzie Strategic Consulting. I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit my website or engage my consulting services.

2. Information I Collect

I may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide when contacting me or engaging my services.
  • Business Information: Information about your organization that you share during our consulting engagement.
  • Website Usage Data: Information about how you use my website, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve my consulting services
  • To respond to your inquiries and communications
  • To send you information about my services that may be of interest to you
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To analyze and improve my website and services

4. Information Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. I may share information with trusted third parties who assist me in operating my website or conducting my business, as long as they agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies

My website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

My website may contain links to third-party websites. I have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information I hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to restrict or object to processing of your information
  • The right to data portability

9. Children's Privacy

My website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective when posted.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at sheamckenziebc@gmail.com.
