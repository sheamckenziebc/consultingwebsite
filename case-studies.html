Market Research & Growth

Government Sector Partnership Development

As a Strategic Development Analyst at Waterworth, I led a comprehensive market research initiative focused on identifying growth opportunities within the government sector.

The Challenge

The organization needed to expand its presence in the government sector but lacked structured data on potential partnership opportunities and market entry points.

The Approach

  • Conducted in-depth analysis of government procurement patterns and decision-making processes
  • Identified key stakeholders and decision-makers within target government agencies
  • Developed a relationship-building framework tailored to the unique needs of public sector entities
  • Created data-driven prioritization model to focus resources on highest-potential opportunities

The Outcome

The research and strategic framework led to targeted relationship-building initiatives that significantly expanded the organization's government sector partnerships. The data-driven approach enabled more efficient resource allocation and higher conversion rates for partnership opportunities.

Industry: Government & Public Sector
Services: Market Research, Strategic Analysis, Partnership Development
